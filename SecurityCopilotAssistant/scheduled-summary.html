Overview

The Scheduled Summary solution provides automated, AI-generated security reports delivered to your team on a configurable schedule. Security Copilot analyzes your security posture, recent incidents, and threat landscape to produce comprehensive, actionable summaries.

This solution includes 5 specialized Logic Apps, each focused on a specific security domain. Deploy them individually based on your needs, or deploy all at once for comprehensive coverage.
