Who I am

Hi!
I'm Mario Cuomo and I'm 24 years old. I have a degree in Computer Engineering - Bachelor Degree - and I am currently a Computer Engineering student - Master - at Roma Tre University in Rome.
I live in Rome but I am originally from Naples, more exactly Pompeii.

I attended a variant of Scientific high school - Scientific high school of Applied Sciences. This varian replaces the hours of study of the Latin language with study hours regarding computer science. I regret it? Sometimes. I'll do it again? Absolutely yes!
During those years I am passionate about the world of Computer Science and I fully thank my professors to send me this passion.
In this regard I love to quote Richard Feyman:

There is no learning without having to pose a question. And a question requires doubt. People search for certainty. But there is no certainty

I like to study and deepen every topic but at the same time I also like to share my knowledge. This year - 2021 - I am following courses to acquire teaching enablement. I think it is a fundamental stage.
I am currently a member of the Unity Student Ambassadors & Microsoft Student Ambassadors program and an active member of the Italian community DotNetCode.
