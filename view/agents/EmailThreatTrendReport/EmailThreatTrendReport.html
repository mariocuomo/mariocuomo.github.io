Email Threat Trend Report

Analyses email threat trends over the past 7 days: spam, phish, malware volumes, top targeted users, and ZAP effectiveness.

Email Security KQL

Skills Used

GetEmailThreatVolumes GetDailyThreatTrend GetTopTargetedUsers GetZAPEffectiveness GetMaliciousUrlClicks

Typical Workflow

  1. Threat Volume by Type — Queries EmailEvents for a breakdown of inbound threats (Phish, Malware, Spam) by delivery action (Blocked, Delivered, Junked).
  2. 7-Day Trend — Retrieves daily threat counts to identify spikes and patterns across the week.
  3. Top Targeted Users — Identifies the 15 users who received the most phishing and malware emails.
  4. ZAP Effectiveness — Analyses Zero-hour Auto Purge remediation activity and success rates by ZAP type.
  5. Malicious URL Clicks — Identifies users who clicked on malicious URLs and whether Safe Links blocked the click-through.
  6. Report Generation — Consolidates all findings into a structured Email Threat Trend Report with recommendations.
